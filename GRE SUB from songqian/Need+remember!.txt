35. A hypothetical boson has a nonzero value for all of the following quantities. 
Which quantity is the same for the boson and its antiparticle? 
(Ignore the existence of weak interactions.)
(A)	Strangeness
(B)	Charm
(C)	Intrinsic parity
(D)	Magnetic moment
(E)	Electric charge
解：选（C）。关于正反粒子，它们的质量、寿命、自旋磁矩的g因子完全相同。
由于由一对正反粒子组成的纯中性体系具有确定的宇称，推出正反Fermion有相
反的内禀宇称，正反Boson有相同的内禀宇称。请记住该结论。
